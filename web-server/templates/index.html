Python Theme Poem

In a world of snakes and bytes,
Where code becomes the guiding light,
Python slithers, swift and clean,
In the kingdom of the machine.

With elegance it does aspire,
To conquer tasks, to never tire,
Its syntax dances, graceful, free,
A language of such majesty.

From data science to web design,
Python's power doth ever shine,
So let us raise our voices high,
To the language that can touch the sky.

O Python, thou art our muse,
In thee, we find our greatest views,
Forever shall our code embrace,
The beauty of thy simple grace.

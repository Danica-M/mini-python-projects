Java Theme Poem

In the land of curly braces and semicolons,
Where objects roam in endless functions,
Java stands tall, robust and strong,
In the realm of code, where it belongs.

With inheritance and polymorphic might,
It weaves together threads of byte,
Exceptional handling, it does bestow,
A language of order, where logic flows.

From enterprise apps to Android's domain,
Java's versatility is plain,
Let us raise our voices, let us sing,
To the language that makes our programs swing.

O Java, thou art our guiding star,
In thee, we find our path afar,
Forever shall our code embrace,
The elegance of thy structured grace.
